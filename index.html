Course Instructor

Dr. P Vijaya Kumar received his B.Sc(2005) and M.Sc (2007) in Chemistry from Scott Christian college Autonomous, Nagercoil affiliated to Manonmanium Sundaranar University TIRUNELVELI. He obtained his Ph.D in Chemistry(2020) under the guidance of Dr. S. Mary Jelastin Kala , Associate Professor, Department of Chemistry, St. Xavier's College Autonomous PALAYAMKOTTAI from Manonmanium Sundaranar University TIRUNELVELI. Apart from this he received his B.Ed, M.Ed and M.Phil in Education from various reputed universities from India. From 2007 to 2019 he worked as Postgraduate Chemistry Teacher/Assistant professor of Chemistry at various reputed institutions in Tamilnadu. From 2019 to 2021 he worked as Principal at an esteemed institution in Kanyakumari district. He received better teacher award from Central Rotary club TIRUNELVELI in 2017.


"I believe students learn Chemistry by doing experiment, and that my classes are a medium through which my students will learn life skills that are critical for their success in college and future careers.
